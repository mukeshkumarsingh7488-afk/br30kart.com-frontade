How BR30 Kart Works

Transparency aur Trust hamari pehli priority hai. 🤝

BR30 Kart is a transparent trading course marketplace where students can easily buy courses and sellers can list verified trading content under BR30 Trader ecosystem.

Powered by BR30 Trader, founded by Mukesh Raj, focusing on trading education, price action and option buying mentorship in India.

For Student 🎓

1

Simple Registration

Apna email verify karein aur seconds mein account banayein.

2

Browse Marketplace

Experts ke courses, PDFs aur tools ko explore karein.

3

Secure Checkout

Razorpay/UPI ke zariye secure payment karein aur instant access payein.

4

Instant Automatic Access ⚡

Jaise hi aapki payment success hogi, course bina kisi deri ke Automatic aapke "My Courses" section mein add ho jayega. No waiting, no manual activation!

5

Learn & Get Certified

Course complete karein aur automatic certificate download karein.

For Sellers 💼

1

Apply for Seller Account

Apni puri details (ID proof, Experience, Trading Style) ke saath apply karein.

2

KYC & Verification

Hamari Admin team aapki details aur content quality manual check karegi.

🛡️ Admin Note: Seller account tabhi active hoga jab hum approval denge.
3

List Your Products

Approval ke baad aap apne Trading Courses aur Assets list kar sakte hain.

4

Track Sales & Earnings

Apne dedicated dashboard se apni sales aur payouts manage karein.
