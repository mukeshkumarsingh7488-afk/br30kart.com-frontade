BR30 Kart FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about BR30 Kart, digital products, sellers, certificates, payments and support.

What is BR30 Kart?

BR30 Kart is a premium digital marketplace where traders and educators can buy and sell trading courses, indicators, eBooks, PDFs, mentorship programs and other educational digital products.

When will I get access after purchasing a course?

Once your payment is successfully completed, your purchased course is usually available instantly inside your account under the My Courses section.

Does BR30 Kart provide trading signals or investment advice?

No. BR30 Kart is an educational marketplace. All products are created for learning purposes only and should not be considered financial or investment advice.

How can I verify my certificate?

Eligible learners receive a digital certificate after course completion. You can verify its authenticity using the official Certificate Verification page and your certificate ID.

What is your refund policy?

Since all products are digital, refunds are generally not available after successful delivery. Exceptional cases such as duplicate payments or verified technical issues are reviewed by our support team.

How can I become a seller?

You can apply through the BR30 Kart Seller Program. Every seller application goes through identity verification, KYC and content quality review before approval.

How do seller payouts work?

Seller payouts are processed according to the BR30 Kart payout policy after applicable platform fees, payment gateway charges and compliance checks.

How can I contact BR30 Kart Support?

You can reach our support team via email at support.br30trader@gmail.com or through WhatsApp at +91 6200986380 .
